ELON MUSK


"It is possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary."

Elon Musk is known for his visionary leadership and innovation in technology and space exploration. He has made significant contributions in the fields of electric vehicles, space travel, and sustainable energy.

Biography

Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa, on June 28, 1971. He developed an early interest in computing and technology, teaching himself computer programming at the age of 12. Musk attended Queen's University in Canada before transferring to the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in physics and economics.

In 1995, Musk moved to California to attend Stanford University but left after two days to pursue a career in business and technology. He co-founded Zip2, an online city guide, which was sold for nearly $300 million in 1999. He then founded X.com, an online payment company that later became PayPal and was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in stock in 2002.
